Background

Armond Arabshahi also know by his stage name as Unlike Pluto, is an American songwrite, singer, DJ and record producer.
He is known for his Pluto Tapes series of albums, where he challenges himself to release one song per week starting from May 2018.
Besides his Unlike Pluto project he has a side project with Hamilton singer Joanna Jones called Why Mona where they create covers of old school classics.

Originally from Atlanta and based in Los Angeles, Unlike Pluto has performed at various music festivals, including Lollapalooza, Bonnaroo, and Euphoria Music Festival.

Pluto's YouTube channel is currently on 266k subscribers as im writing this and is still releasing music weekly.

Unliek Pluto currently has released 3 albums names Pluto Tapes: Volume 1, 2 and 3 with a large quantity of non-album singles adding to the collection weekly.
