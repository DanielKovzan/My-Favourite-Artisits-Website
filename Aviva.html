Background

Aviva Anastasia Payne also known as AVIVA, is an Australian singer-songwriter with aspirations of also becoming an author.
AVIVA plans to release a book named SELF/LESS in 2021 in which she creates a world where "creativity and self-expression are illegal".
She was also presented with an opportunity to play for 5 shows on Poppy's "Am I a Girl?" Tour.

AVIVA's album, VOMULE 1 was released in 2020, featuring all of her songs to date, here's a link to her playlist featuring all 11 songs

BLAME IT ON THE KIDS

BLAME IT ON THE KIDS was premiered on the 15th of November 2018 with a view count of 1,120,620 and a total view count of around 40 million.
